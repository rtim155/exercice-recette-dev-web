Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a small bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Add eggs: Once the butter is melted and sizzling, pour in the egg mixture.
  4. Cook the edges: As the eggs begin to set, gently push the cooked edges toward the center, tilting the pan so that the uncooked egg runs underneath.
  5. Add filling (optional): When the eggs are nearly set but still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your choice of cheese and fillings over one half of the omelette.
  6. Fold and serve: Carefully fold the omelette in half. Slide it onto a plate and serve immediately.
